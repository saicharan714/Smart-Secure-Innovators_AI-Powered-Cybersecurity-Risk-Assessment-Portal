## INSTRUCTIONS FOR COMPLETION

1. **Complete honestly and fairly** - Your assessment should reflect your genuine observations
2. **Provide specific examples** - Don't just rate; explain with concrete examples
3. **Be constructive** - Even critical feedback should be helpful and professional
4. **Separate person from performance** - Evaluate work quality, not personal characteristics
5. **Consider all phases** - Reflect on the full project duration
6. **Be specific** - Vague comments are less helpful than specific observations
7. **Maintain confidentiality** - Treat this as a confidential evaluation
8. **Submit on time** - Complete and submit by the deadline
